Addmission Process

Registration for new admission is open from the month of January every year. Parents are requested to register their ward accordingly. The students will have to undergo Entrance Test for it.

For registration, Prospectus & Admission Form can be collected from the School Office on any working day during office hours on payment of Rs.1500/- [One thousand five hundred only] being registration fees.

SUBJECTS FOR ENTRANCE EXAM :

NURSERY TO STD. II : No Test is required.
III TO VI : English, Hindi & Maths.
VII TO IX : English, Hindi, Maths & Science

ADMISSION FOR CLASS XI

Class XI admissions will be based on the Board Results. Students can opt for the subjects in the following manner. The course and the criteria for admission in different streams will be strictly based on cut off % of that particular year.
